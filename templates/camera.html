How to Use
  1. Allow camera access when your browser asks
  2. Make sure the leaf is clearly visible in the camera frame
  3. Good lighting is important for accurate detection
  4. Click "Capture & Analyze" to take a photo and get results
  5. The result will appear in the right panel with disease information
Troubleshooting
  • Camera not working? Check your browser permissions and allow camera access
  • Poor camera quality? Ensure adequate lighting and keep the device steady
  • Blurry results? Allow the camera to auto-focus before capturing
  • Still having issues? Try using the upload method instead
